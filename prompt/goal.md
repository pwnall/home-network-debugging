# Goal

This investigation aims to get the Google Nest WiFi Pro units on my network to
use their Ethernet connections through a switch to form a wired backhaul, in
order to reduce wireless contention.

My backhaul validation process below shows that this is not currently the case.

1. Open the Google Home app on an Android device
2. Find one of the Google Nest WiFi Pro units and click on it
3. Click on the settings cog
4. Look up the "Connection type:" entry under "Wi-Fi Information"
5. Declare success if the type is not "Wireless"
